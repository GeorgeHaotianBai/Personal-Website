I am an incoming PhD student in the <a href="https://mitsloan.mit.edu/faculty/academic-groups/ties/about-us" target="_blank" rel="noopener">TIES group</a> at the <a href="https://mitsloan.mit.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">MIT Sloan School of Management</a>. My research studies technological change, innovation, and labour markets, together with their connections to political economy and strategy. I am particularly interested in geoeconomics, legal and political institutions, and the use of agentic AI in social science.

Before MIT, I completed an M.Phil. in Economics in the <a href="https://www.economics.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Department of Economics</a> at the <a href="https://www.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">University of Oxford</a>. During my time at Oxford, I was a member of <a href="https://www.lmh.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lady Margaret Hall</a>. I also hold a B.A. in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics.
