I am an incoming PhD student in the TIES group at the MIT Sloan School of Management. My research studies technological change, innovation, and labour markets, together with their connections to political economy and strategy. I am particularly interested in geoeconomics, legal and political institutions, and the use of agentic AI in social science.

Before MIT, I completed an M.Phil. in Economics in the <a href="https://www.economics.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Department of Economics</a> at the <a href="https://www.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">University of Oxford</a>. During my time at Oxford, I was a member of <a href="https://www.lmh.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lady Margaret Hall</a>. I also hold a B.A. in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics.
